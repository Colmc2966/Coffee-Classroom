Kenya

Our bean of the week this week is Kenya. This bean is one of the worlds most renowned beans for its juicy and complex nature. With tastes of fruity citrus leading to its beautifully sweet tastes. This bean is generally not mixed and made to a medium roast leading to a medium acidity and body.
