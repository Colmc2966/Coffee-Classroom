FAQS about Events


Why do we host events?


The main purpose of our events is to promote a helpful and active community! We realise that not everyone is lucky enough to have a broad social circle so we aim to bring a community of like minded similar people together online! Especially after covid, alot of our users have formed together to become a tight knit supportive community who have gotten to know each other over our events from the past year.


How are we notified of the events?


Depending on the event, you will be sent an email with all instructions and details of the specific event. Sometimes if you want to immerse yourself in our events you will need some extra materials, these emails will outline everything you will need in order to get the best experience from our events.


Is there a charge for these events?


We do not charge for anything! We might suggest equipment that will enhance your experience throughout our events but we will never ask directly for any form of currency.
